About COVID


%COVID is an app that employs machine learning to help people make better decisions on the level of treatment they need, based on their observed symptoms and known chronic diseases.

Data Source

The data for this app is provided by Carbon Health, a tech-enabled healthcare provider based in San Francisco, CA, USA. It can be accessed from Carbon Health's Github Repository.

Carbon Health is requesting data from outpatient test centers and inpatient healthcare facilities which are treating COVID-19. It has provided templates and instructions on their project page for submissions to support their initiative.


The App

The %COVID app is built on a machine learning model trained on clinical characteristics of patients who have taken a COVID-19 test. It uses only information that individuals have on hand, enabling them to assess if they potentially have COVID-19 and decide if they should see a doctor to get tested as soon as possible.
